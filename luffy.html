About Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the main protagonist of the One Piece manga and anime series created by Eiichiro Oda. He is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and possesses the power of the Gum-Gum Fruit, which grants him rubber-like abilities.

Luffy is known for his cheerful personality, love for adventure, and his goal to become the Pirate King by finding the One Piece, the greatest treasure in the world.
