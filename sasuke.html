About Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is a central character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is a skilled ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village and one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha clan.

Sasuke is driven by a desire for revenge against his older brother, Itachi Uchiha, who slaughtered their entire clan. Throughout the series, Sasuke's journey involves seeking power, redemption, and ultimately finding his own path as a ninja.
