Roronoa Zoro

About roronoa zoro

Roronoa Zoro, also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is a swordsman and a member of the Straw Hat Pirates led by Monkey D. Luffy in the One Piece series. He is known for his incredible swordsmanship and dedication to becoming the world's greatest swordsman.

Zoro is often seen wielding three swords simultaneously and specializes in a unique sword-fighting style called Santoryu or "Three Sword Style." He is a loyal and stoic character who values honor and has a strong sense of justice.
